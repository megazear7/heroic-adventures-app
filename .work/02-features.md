# Races

## Race: Human Features
Race ID: 4sNwKfUyQv2Irg1wE4xIah

### Human Feature: Deflect
Use this heroic action *after* rolling an agility defense roll. Increase your block to 5 for that attack.

### Human Feature: Team Combat
Your initiative is treated as being equal to the initiative of the ally with the next highest initiative.

### Human Feature: Skill Versatility
Gain 3 expertise training.

### Human Feature: Heroism
At the start of combat, if all of your heroic actions are spent, regain a heroic action. Requires level 5+.

### Human Feature: Tactics
When you use a major action, an adjacent ally can immediately use a heroic action.

### Human Feature: Adaptability
Reroll action checks when the roll on the dice is equal to your training in that action check. For example, if you have 3 training in grappling and you roll a 2, you may reroll.

## Race: Elf Features
Race ID: 6sa5V9r1vhRypqnhz0MiCd

### Elf Feature: Battle Lore
Make an intelligence vs. intelligence attack with a weapon. Requires high elf or half elf.

### Elf Feature: Woodland Lore
Make an initiative vs. initiative attack with a weapon. Requires wood elf.

### Elf Feature: Woodland Tactics
Gain 2 block when not using a shield when fighting in a woodland environment. If you are using a shield, increase the provided block by 1. Requires wood elf.

### Elf Feature: Swift (Elf)
Increase your major movement speed by 1. Requires level 7+ if not wood elf.

### Elf Feature: Arcane Lore
Acquire 2 mystic spells and gain 2 mana. Lose 1 skill. Requires gray elf or high elf.

### Elf Feature: Wild Lore
Acquire 2 nature spells and gain 2 mana. Lose 1 skill. Requires wood elf.

### Elf Feature: Essential Power
Gain 3 mana. Requires high elf or grey elf.

### Elf Feature: World Knowledge
Gain expertise in three different actions.

### Elf Feature: Of The World
Gain expertise in three different actions. Requires half elf.

### Elf Feature: Ancient Mending
Provide 1d20 healing to an adjacent target that is not yourself. Requires grey elf, half elf, or high elf and willpower 3+.

## Race: Dwarf Features
Race ID: 3wJHYh4grqHtglOnEPFam8

### Dwarf Feature: Persistence
Make a charge attack without drawing opportunity.

### Dwarf Feature: Pummel
Make a willpower vs. willpower attack that does 1d4 + willpower damage that ignores damage reduction.

### Dwarf Feature: Life of the underground
Gain 1 toughness.

### Dwarf Feature: Mining Dwarf
Gain 1 willpower.

### Dwarf Feature: Rune Lore (Dwarf)
Acquire 2 rune spells and gain 2 mana. Lose 1 strength.

### Dwarf Feature: Rune Power (Dwarf)
Gain 3 mana.

### Dwarf Feature: Spell Ward
When hit by a spell but before the damage is rolled, spend a heroic action to halve the damage.

### Dwarf Feature: Master Craftsmen
When purchasing an artisan or mastercrafted item, spend 1d20 days crafting it instead. The total cost is reduced by 100 for artisan items and 500 for mastercrafted items.

## Race: Halfling Features
Race ID: 49FS9F9lqDFJEhO6EBqeBD

### Halfling Feature: Unseen
Make an attack with advantage.

### Halfling Feature: Dodge and weave
Make a major move without drawing an opportunity attack.

### Halfling Feature: Hamstring
Make an agility vs. agility attack against a creature that does damage equal to your strength that cannot be ignored. If the attack hits, that creature cannot take major actions until the beginning of the next round.

### Halfling Feature: Woodland Roots
Acquire 2 nature spells and gain 2 mana. Lose 1 agility.

### Halfling Feature: Woodland Power
Gain 3 mana.

### Halfling Feature: Nimble Escape
You may move through creatures that are larger than you.

### Halfling Feature: Halfling Luck
When you fail an attack roll, defense roll, or expertise check, you may roll again and gain advantage to the new roll.

### Halfling Feature: Master cook
Spend 3 coins to purchase from a market and provide a meal for the entire party. Double the healing everyone receives from resting that day.

## Race: Gnome Features
Race ID: 6ySfWsUQ7qeMKBFAKsRiKI

### Gnome Feature: Dodge
Use this heroic action when being attacked. The attacker must use their intelligence for the attack roll instead of their skill.

### Gnome Feature: Rune Lore (Gnome)
Acquire 2 rune spells and gain 2 mana. Lose 1 strength.

### Gnome Feature: Rune Power (Gnome)
Gain 3 mana.

### Gnome Feature: Percipience
Gain 1 intelligence.

### Gnome Feature: Tool Making
Spend 1 hour making a tool or trap that can give advantage to an expertise check or can do up to 1d6 damage that ignores damage reduction.

### Gnome Feature: Tinkerer
Each time you level up with class advancement, including the time you chose this feature, you may create one artisan item. Requires level 5.

### Gnome Feature: Master Tinkerer
Each time you level up with class advancement, including the time you chose this feature, you may create one mastercrafted item. Requires level 9 and **Tinkerer**.

## Race: Half Orc Features
Race ID: 4BhSvJHNtVeIFK6v7rVoW

### Half Orc Feature: Tooth and claw
Make a skill vs. willpower melee attack. The attack does 1d4 damage + strength that ignores armor.

### Half Orc Feature: Ruthless
Use this heroic action after killing a creature. Make a charge attack without drawing opportunity.

### Half Orc Feature: Furious
Use this heroic action after killing a creature. Make an attack against an adjacent creature.

### Half Orc Feature: Determined
Gain block 1 while not equipped with a shield.

### Half Orc Feature: Outcast
Gain advantage when 4 or more spaces away from all allies.

### Half Orc Feature: Wisdom of the Clan
Intelligence attacks made against a creature that you already attacked this encounter gain advantage.

# Classes

## Class: Wizard Features
Class ID: 5dYbCbOTdP9q4m8MnNGG9m

### Wizard Feature: Power
Cast a spell and apply a single enhancement at no mana cost.

### Wizard Feature: Quick Cast
When a new initiative card is drawn but before any creatures take an action, you may cast a spell.

### Wizard Feature: Combat casting
You may wield a staff or wand one handed as a secondary weapon and cast spells even when having another weapon equipped as a primary weapon. Do not draw opportunity attacks when preparing and casting spells.

### Wizard Feature: Warrior Casting
Cast spells even while you are not equipped with a staff or wand.

### Wizard Feature: Armored Casting
Cast spells even while wearing medium armor.

### Wizard Feature: Greater Armored Casting
Cast spells even while wearing heavy armor or a shield. Requires **Armored Casting**.

### Wizard Feature: Spell practice
Acquire 2 mystic spells.

### Wizard Feature: Source
Gain 3 mana.

### Wizard Feature: Greater Source
Gain 3 mana. Requires **Source**.

### Wizard Feature: Guild Apprentice
Acquire 1 mystic spell. You may spend 1 hour to make a roll on the mages guild table without being at the mage's guild.

### Wizard Feature: Archmage
Acquire 1 mystic spell. You may modify the result of a mage's guild roll up or down by 3. Requires **Scholar** and intelligence 5+.

## Class: Warlock Features
Class ID: 4LoaRA5d4g0ezTkLrVIQDh

### Warlock Feature: Unleashed Anger
Cast a spell and add 1 to your hex dice.

### Warlock Feature: Controlled Tempest
When casting a spell, reduce the damage you take by half.

### Warlock Feature: Arcane Greed
When casting a spell, you may reroll 1s on your hex dice.

### Warlock Feature: Greater Hex
Increase your hex dice to 1d6. Requires level 5+.

### Warlock Feature: Infernal Hex
Increase your hex dice to 1d8. Requires Greater Hex and level 13+.

### Warlock Feature: Warlock Training
Acquire 1 mystic spell.

## Class: Rogue Features
Class ID: 6JEYHX0mvw6r97Xykjg2mM

### Rogue Feature: Back stab
Make an attack against a creature you are flanking and gain an extra weapon damage dice.

### Rogue Feature: Quick Strike
Make an initiative vs. initiative attack.

### Rogue Feature: Set Trap
Place a marker in a space up to 3 spaces away and choose one of the below effects. If any creature moves into or out of the space with the marker or an adjacent space, make a skill vs. agility roll and remove the marker. If the attack hits, apply the chosen effect. You may use this ability outside of combat without counting the heroic action as being used.

**Poison dart**: 1d6 damage that ignores armor at the start of each round. If you roll a 5 or 6 for damage, the effect ends.

**Sticky slime**: The target cannot make major moves until they spend a major action and succeed on a strength vs. skill check against you.

**Arrow**: The target takes 2d6 damage.

### Rogue Feature: Two weapon fighting
Attack with your secondary weapon. In order to use this feature, you must have two weapons equipped and both must be small.

### Rogue Feature: Twin weapon fighting
Attack with your secondary weapon. In order to use this feature, you must have two weapons equipped and your off hand weapon must be small. Requires Two weapon fighting.

### Rogue Feature: Weak Spot
If you are flanking an enemy creature, you may place a single d4 on this action. The number of d4s that can be on this feature at the same time is equal to your heroic actions per day limit. The next back stab heroic action you take against a creature, you may spend these d4 to add to your skill roll or damage roll. You can only use this ability against one creature at a time. Requires: Back stab

### Rogue Feature: Trap Expert
Increase the damage of your traps by one damage dice and place two traps each time you take the action. Requires skill 5+.

### Rogue Feature: Exploit
Gain +3 with advantage instead of +2

### Rogue Feature: Dualist
Gain advantage with agility against melee attacks when neither you nor the attacker are adjacent to an ally

## Class: Priest Features
Class ID: 61gej2tBjATDVrylKRtuFL

### Priest Feature: Orison
Any other character with a prepared spell may immediately apply the spell effect.

### Priest Feature: Inspiring Presence
Choose a character within line of sight. They may take a heroic action that does not count towards their limit of heroic actions per day.

### Priest Feature: Benevolence
Any ally within line of sight that is within melee range of an enemy may make a major move without drawing opportunity attacks. They must not end their movement adjacent to an enemy.

### Priest Feature: Vespers
Add d6 to the healing of any spell you cast that already heals.

### Priest Feature: Ex Nihilo
Add 4 to the range of all spells. Any spell that has a range of adjacent instead has a range of 4.

## Class: Druid Features
Class ID: 5KzZZDN7g2WPjce3eXpnth

### Druid Feature: Force of Nature
Make a weapon attack. The damage is equal to your full strength (no damage dice) and ignores armor.

### Druid Feature: Winds of Nature
Cast a major spell and apply the effect immediately without waiting on the spell card.

### Druid Feature: Incarnation of Nature
Once per day, transform into an animal of your choice. While transformed, you have stats equal to the beast monster profile of your current level. If you are reduced to 0 health while transformed, you return to your normal body with 1 health remaining. You may return to your normal body with a major action. The next time the sun sets or rises, you return to your normal body regardless with whatever health you had before transforming. While transformed, you may not use any of your class features or cast spells.

### Druid Feature: Ally of the Forest
Gain 2 agility when in woods or forests.

### Druid Feature: Life of Nature
Gain 4 health.

## Class: Fighter Features
Class ID: 5iDGifi7SmV7vJ3zxqgRQ7

### Fighter Feature: Second wind
Gain temporary health equal to 1d6 + willpower. This replaces any temporary health that you already had.

### Fighter Feature: Cleave
Make a melee attack action with a weapon and target two creatures that you are adjacent to and roll damage for whichever creature you hit. If your attack roll was higher than both defense rolls you may choose which of the two creatures, you hit.

### Fighter Feature: Bull Rush
Charge a creature and gain an extra weapon dice of damage. You must start more than 4 spaces away from the target.

### Fighter Feature: Shield Bash
If you are using a shield, make a willpower vs. willpower attack with damage equal to d6 + your strength.

### Fighter Feature: Threaten
If an enemy creature was adjacent to you at any point during this turn and attacked an adjacent ally, you may attack that enemy creature.

### Fighter Feature: Aim
After rolling damage you may choose to reroll any dice that rolled a 1. You cannot reroll dice after rolling for exploding damage.

### Fighter Feature: Greater aim
After rolling damage you may choose to reroll any dice that rolled a 1 or a 2. The dice must be d6 or larger. You cannot reroll dice after rolling for exploding damage. Required aim.

## Class: Barbarian Features
Class ID: 34JpESBWXyBg0QwL7J1B6g

### Barbarian Feature: Slam
You may jump 3 spaces without drawing an opportunity attack and make a melee attack with a two-handed weapon. While jumping, you may leap over any human sized creature. If the target is killed, any remaining damage can be applied to any creature adjacent to the target.

### Barbarian Feature: Flurry
Spend 1 fury dice and make two attacks, one with your primary weapon and one with your secondary weapon.

### Barbarian Feature: Rage
Gain a fury dice and attack with your secondary weapon.

### Barbarian Feature: Unleashed Pain
Increase the size of your fury dice to d6.

### Barbarian Feature: Great Strength
You may wield a two-handed weapon with one hand and a shield or other weapon in the other hand. Requires a strength of 6.

### Barbarian Feature: Titanic Strength
You may wield two two-handed weapons. On all heroic actions, attack with both weapons. Requires Great Strength and a strength of 8.

## Class: Cleric Features
Class ID: 2u2GEPE0dlPLK30QK7wjU2

### Cleric Feature: Inspiration
Choose an adjacent ally. They may make an attack.

### Cleric Feature: Leadership (Cleric)
Choose an ally with more remaining health than you. They may take a heroic action that does not count towards their limit of heroic actions per day.

### Cleric Feature: Sacrifice (Cleric)
Choose an adjacent ally. If your damage reduction is higher than theirs, they use your damage reduction. When they take damage, half of all the damage they take is applied to your character instead. This continues until you choose to end the effect or until a round ends where you and the target are not adjacent.

### Cleric Feature: Direction
Make a minor movement and then choose an adjacent ally. They may also make a minor movement.

### Cleric Feature: Turn Undead
When casting a spell apply all the healing as damage that ignores damage reduction. All the targets must be undead.

### Cleric Feature: Greater Turn Undead
When using Turn Undead, reroll the lowest damage dice. Requires Turn Undead.

## Class: Paladin Features
Class ID: 43uhTVmrpwNhSVojexmRpM

### Paladin Feature: Arbitrate
Make a melee willpower vs. willpower attack that does 1d6 + willpower damage armor.

### Paladin Feature: Sacrifice (Paladin)
Take damage that ignores armor equal to your willpower. Do the same amount of damage to up to three enemy creatures within 3 spaces.

### Paladin Feature: Servitude
Take damage that ignores armor equal to your willpower. Heal the same amount of damage to up to 3 allies within 3 spaces.

### Paladin Feature: Humble
Use this heroic action when being attacked. Do not roll a defense roll. You are automatically hit by the attack. Whatever damage you take, do the same damage to up to three enemy creatures within 3 spaces.

### Paladin Feature: Holy Order
Acquire 2 divine spells and 2 mana.

### Paladin Feature: Sacred Mastery
Acquire 2 divine spells and 2 mana. Requires **Holy Order**.

### Paladin Feature: Divine Discipline
Gain 3 mana. Requires Holy Training.

## Class: Ranger Features
Class ID: 60ROr9ryrCYHYDV0dPweRG

### Ranger Feature: Finish the Hunt
Make an initiative vs. initiative attack against your prey and add damage to the attack equal to the number of hunting points you have.

### Ranger Feature: Ploy of the Ranger
Make an initiative vs. initiative attack against your prey and add to your attack roll equal to the number of hunting points you have.

### Ranger Feature: Kill Shot
If you make an attack and kill your prey, make another attack against a new target.

### Ranger Feature: Stalk the Prey
Increase your maximum hunting points equal to your initiative.

### Ranger Feature: Ranger Resolve
Gain temporary health equal to your initiative + your current hunting points.

### Ranger Feature: Animal Companion
Choose an animal companion: dog, wolf, mountain lion, panther, or an equivalent animal approved by your game master. Your pet has stats equal to a minion creature profile of your character level except that it always acts on initiative 1. If your pet ever dies, you may spend 1 week of dedicated in-game time acquiring a new pet of your choice from the below list. You and the game master can use this time to roleplay how you come about acquiring a new pet.

### Ranger Feature: Claw and Arrow
Your animal companion gains damage against your prey equal to your hunting points. Requires Animal Companion.

### Ranger Feature: Ranger Bond
Gain 1 hunting point when your animal companion attacks your prey. Requires Animal Companion.

### Ranger Feature: Companions Fury
Your animal companion has stats equal to a soldier creature profile. It still activates on the initiative 1. Requires Animal Companion
